Desktop Features

  • Deal Cards: Click deck or press D
  • Reveal Cards: Click on cards or press:
    • 1 - Left slot
    • 2 - Top slot
    • 3 - Right slot
    • 4 - Bottom slot
    • 5 - Center slot
  • Reset Cards: Press R to clear all slots
  • Right-Click Menu:
    • Deal New Card: Replaces with random card
    • Choose from Menu: Select specific card
  • Empty Slot Menu:
    • Right-click empty slot to choose a specific card
  • Tooltips:
    • Hover over empty slot or unrevealed cards for slot descriptions
    • Hover over revealed cards for card descriptions
  • Toggle Text Descriptions: Press T
  • Fullscreen: Click fullscreen button or press F

Touchscreen Features

  • Deal Cards: Tap deck
  • Show slot description:
    • Tap empty slot
    • Two finger press on unrevealed card
  • Empty Slot Menu:
    • Two finger press on empty slot to choose a specific card
  • Show card description:
    • Tap to reveal card
  • Deal new card or choose new card:
    • Two finger press on revealed card
  • Hide text:
    • Click anywhere on screen
